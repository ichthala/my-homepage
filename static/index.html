Hello! My name is Alice.

I am a Ruby developer, theatre artist, and writer. I've previously worked as a software engineer at Constant Contact and currently work at Contently.

You can see a talk I gave about mentorship in software engineering teams here, or read an article on the topic here.

I am one of the Artistic Directors of Torn Out Theater, a small nonprofit theater company that aims to do absolutely nothing the traditional way.

In May of 2016, I co-directed an all-female, mostly-nude production of The Tempest with my good friend Pitr Strait. The production celebrated body positivity and free self-expression, and was covered in the Guardian, Salon, NBC News, the Huffington Post, Jezebel, the Atlantic, and many more. The two performances were attended by over 400 people, and the production was later restaged in Prospect Park.

If you want to get me talking, try asking about Thomas Ligotti or the Enneagram.
